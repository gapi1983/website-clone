M1 Chip Specs

M1 Chip of the future.

It’s here. Our first chip designed specifically for Mac. Packed with an astonishing 16 billion transistors,The M1 has four high-performance "Firestorm" and four energy-efficient "Icestorm" cores, providing a hybrid configuration similar to ARM DynamIQ and Intel's Lakefield and Alder Lake processors.[10] This combination allows power-use optimizations not possible with previous Apple–Intel architecture devices.The M1 integrates an Apple designed[16] eight-core (seven in some base models) graphics processing unit (GPU).
